frequently asked questions

how to cancel?

To cancel your reservation, please contact our reservations team directly via phone or email. Kindly provide your booking details for prompt assistance.

is there any vacancy?

To check for vacancy, please visit our website's booking portal or contact our reservations team directly. We're here to assist you promptly.

what are payment methods?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and cash upon check-in. Online payment options are also available.

how to claim coupons codes?

To claim coupon codes, enter the code during the booking process on our website. Alternatively, provide the code when contacting our reservations team.

what are the age requirements?

Our age requirements vary depending on the type of reservation. Please refer to our policies or contact us directly for specific details.
